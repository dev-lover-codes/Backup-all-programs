# Local Doc Viewer - Your Private, Offline Document Manager

Easily import, view, and organize your local documents directly in your browser. Local Doc Viewer acts as a secure, offline-first library for your PDFs, Word documents, and text files.

## üöÄ Key Features

*   **Offline Access**: All files are stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. No internet connection required.
*   **Universal Viewer**:
    *   **PDFs**: High-fidelity rendering with zoom and page navigation.
    *   **Word (.docx)**: View and perform basic edits on Word documents.
    *   **Text & Markdown**: Built-in support for .txt and .md files with clean formatting.
*   **Powerful Editor**:
    *   Rich text editing for DOCX and Text files.
    *   Customize fonts, sizes, colors, and alignment.
    *   Insert images, links, and tables.
    *   Find & Replace functionality.
*   **Dark Mode**: One-click toggle between Light and Dark themes for comfortable reading.
*   **Export Options**: Save your changes and export documents as PDF, DOCX, or TXT.

## üìÅ Supported Formats
*   Microsoft Word (.docx)
*   PDF Documents (.pdf)
*   Text Files (.txt)
*   Markdown (.md)

## üîí Privacy & Security
**Your data never leaves your device.**
Local Doc Viewer operates entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or document contents to external servers. Everything stays strictly within your local browser environment.

1.  Click the extension icon to open your document library.
2.  Drag and drop files or click "Import" to add documents.
