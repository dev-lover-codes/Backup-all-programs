# Privacy Policy - ChatGPT Export Pro

**Last Updated**: December 13, 2024

## Overview

ChatGPT Export Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with a **privacy-first, local-only** approach.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your data.**

### What We Don't Do
- ❌ We don't send your conversations to any external servers
- ❌ We don't track your usage or analytics
- ❌ We don't collect personal information
- ❌ We don't share data with third parties
- ❌ We don't use cookies or tracking pixels
- ❌ We don't monetize your data

## How Your Data Is Handled

### Local Processing
- All conversation extraction happens **locally in your browser**
- All exports are generated **on your device**
- No internet connection required for core functionality

### Local Storage
When you save conversations:
- They are stored in **Chrome's local storage** on your device
- Only you have access to this data
- Data never leaves your computer
- You can delete saved conversations anytime

### Exported Files
- Generated entirely on your computer
- Saved to your Downloads folder
- Never uploaded or transmitted anywhere

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests only necessary permissions:

### activeTab
- **Purpose**: Read ChatGPT conversation content
- **Scope**: Only on chat.openai.com and chatgpt.com
- **Usage**: To extract conversation text for export
- **Data Access**: Current tab content when active

### storage
- **Purpose**: Save your preferences and conversations
- **Scope**: Chrome's local storage API
- **Usage**: Store export settings and saved conversations
- **Location**: Your computer only

### contextMenus
- **Purpose**: Add right-click export options
- **Scope**: ChatGPT pages only
- **Usage**: Provide convenient export shortcuts

### downloads
- **Purpose**: Save exported files
- **Scope**: Chrome's download API
- **Usage**: Download PDF, Markdown, HTML files

### scripting
- **Purpose**: Inject export button into ChatGPT
- **Scope**: ChatGPT domains only
- **Usage**: Add floating export button to page
